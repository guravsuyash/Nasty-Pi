Services

Back office support for SME’s

We support small to medium sized enterprise’s (SME’s) which are looking to expand their business online and become online merchants. We offer a full range of back-office services to support your journey as a growing e-commerce business. Our services will support you for each stage of the company’s development, from inception and early stage concept to helping you manage and grow your business online as a going concern moving forwards.

get started
Start-up capital

Explore funding options and support to turn your idea into a reality. We will help ensure that you have enough funding for your first year of operation examining all associated costs.

Vertically integrated facilities

Operating as a vertically integrated facility by working with us will allow you to have increased profit margins by outsourcing the main back-office workload.

Back Office Support

Boost the scale of your business with outsourced teams which will take care of back-office duties, allowing you to focus on the core needs of your business. We will help you to find a solution that fits your workflow, needs and budget.

Treasury Management

Manage your cashflow efficiently and securely with treasury management services. This will help to streamline your business by managing cash, investments and other financial assets.

Ongoing support through partnerships


Unlike other back-office solution firms, we do not just work with you at the beginning of your venture and then leave you to it - we remain a committed member of your team.

We build lasting relationships with all our clients and continue to work with them in a model which is more similar to a joint venture than a one-off deal. Working closely with our clients on a long-term basis is the foundation of our business ethos. We take great pride in getting companies off the ground to realise their e-commerce vision and then continue to work with them to ensure that their original ideas not only meet but exceed expectations.
